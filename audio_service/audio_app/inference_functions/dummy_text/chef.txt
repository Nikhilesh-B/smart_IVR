At the helm of our kitchen is Executive Chef Marco Ricci, trained in Naples but with a heart firmly planted in Sydney. Marco's two-decade culinary journey, encompassing rustic Italian kitchens and bustling Sydney eateries, is evident in every bite at Nikhilesh's. His passion for pizza is matched only by his love for fusion, blending authentic Italian techniques with Aussie ingredients. Complementing him is a team of talented sous chefs and servers, each bringing a unique flavour to our family, ensuring your dining experience is nothing short of perfection.
